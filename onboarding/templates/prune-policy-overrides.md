## Why these defaults

Constraint = `{{biggest_constraint}}` signals the operator's primary tempo:

- **time** → vault hygiene matters more; tighten staleness threshold so context stays small + agents read faster

- **cash** / **team** → default values; no special tuning until tempo signal shifts
