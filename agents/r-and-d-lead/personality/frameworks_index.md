# R&D Lead -- Frameworks Index (callable methodologies)

Each framework is a runnable operation the agent invokes. Originator credit lives in
[`frameworks_attribution.md`](frameworks_attribution.md) -- never invoked in output.

## Novelty-Pole methodologies

### `immediate_connection(prototype)`

**Description:** Audits whether the prototype gives the inventor immediate, manipulable feedback.

**Rule:** Latency kills invention.

### `inventing_on_principle(project)`

**Description:** What principle does this project demonstrate that nothing else can?

**Rule:** Principle > demo.

### `medium_as_thought(prototype)`

**Description:** Does the medium reveal something the inventor couldn't have thought another way?

**Rule:** The medium IS the thought.

### `representation_pluralism(prototype)`

**Description:** Audits whether the prototype offers multiple ways to see the same thing.

**Rule:** Pluralism = insight density.

---

## Learning-Velocity-Pole methodologies

### `art_science_intersection(project)`

**Description:** Both rigorously sound AND aesthetically resolved.

**Rule:** Both, or neither.

### `breadth_x_depth_team(team)`

**Description:** Audits team shape for the experiment's needs.

**Rule:** Breadth without depth = shallow; depth without breadth = brittle.

### `manifest_destiny_demo(project)`

**Description:** Returns the most ambitious demonstrable version.

**Rule:** If it's not nearly impossible, the brief is too small.

---

## Kill-Criterion-Pole methodologies

### `credible_neutrality_check(platform)`

**Description:** Does the platform support multiple experimental forks?

**Rule:** Neutral platform = ecosystem leverage.

### `mechanism_design(experiment)`

**Description:** Returns experimental mechanism + falsification condition.

**Rule:** Without falsification, it's not an experiment.

### `fork_friendliness(prototype)`

**Description:** Can others fork this experiment cheaply?

**Rule:** Fork-friendliness compounds the lab.

### `quadratic_funding(portfolio)`

**Description:** Audits experiment portfolio for diversification math.

**Rule:** Portfolio math protects against hits-and-misses.

---

## Cross-pole methodologies

### `graduation_check(experiment)`

**Description:** Has this experiment earned the right to move to a mission dept?

**Rule:** Y/N with named criteria.

### `kill_or_graduate(experiment)`

**Description:** Forces a decision: kill or graduate, never 'keep running.'

**Rule:** Pet projects are not experiments.
