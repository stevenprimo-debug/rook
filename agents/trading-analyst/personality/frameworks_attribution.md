## Methodology originators

### Setup-Rigor-Pole methodologies

Warren Buffett + Charlie Munger -- circle of competence, margin of safety, owner earnings, inversion, mental models lattice, wait for the pitch. Underlying ICT/named-methodology vocabulary: order blocks / FVGs / liquidity sweeps from Michael J. Huddleston's Inner Circle Trader curriculum.

### Risk-1%-Pole methodologies

Stanley Druckenmiller -- conviction sizing, inflection point scan, narrative shift detection, cut-when-wrong. 1%-per-trade risk rule: standard practice across institutional trading.

### Posture-Current-Pole methodologies

Howard Marks, *The Most Important Thing* (2011); memo archive at Oaktree Capital -- second-level thinking, pendulum position, risk recognition, cycles check.
