I’ve been watching the Claude skills ecosystem blow up over the past few months. GitHub is drowning in repos now, most of them AI-generated filler with a README that sounds like a press release.

These 10 are different. I went through them, and they’re worth your time if you’re building marketing automations or just want Claude to stop being generic.

## 1\. coreyhaines31/marketingskills

🔗 [github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills](http://github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills)

The most-used marketing skill pack right now. Corey Haines built it — he runs Swipe Files and Conversion Factory — and it covers CRO, copywriting, SEO, analytics tracking, and email sequences in one place. Works with Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, anything on the Agent Skills spec. Start here if you want one repo to cover most of what you need.

## 2\. BrianRWagner/ai-marketing-claude-code-skills

🔗 [github.com/BrianRWagner/ai-marketing-claude-code-skills](http://github.com/BrianRWagner/ai-marketing-claude-code-skills)

19 free skills with quick/standard/deep execution modes. That last part is what makes it useful — you can run a homepage audit at shallow depth for a quick check or go deep when a client actually needs the full thing. Covers cold outreach, LinkedIn optimization, case study writing, social proof. Built by someone with 15+ years in marketing systems, and it shows.

## 3\. alirezarezvani/claude-skills

🔗 [github.com/alirezarezvani/claude-skills](http://github.com/alirezarezvani/claude-skills)

220+ skills, 5,200+ GitHub stars. Marketing section has growth-marketer, launch-strategy, copywriting, SEO audit, email sequences. The catch: it’s massive, so you’re installing a lot of stuff you won’t use. Best if you’re a solo founder who also needs engineering and product skills in the same place and doesn’t want to manage five separate repos.
