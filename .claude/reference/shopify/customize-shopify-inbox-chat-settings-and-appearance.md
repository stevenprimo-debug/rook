You can customize the appearance of the chat, the instant answers that are available to your customers, and the pre-chat form that displays to your customers.

## Turn on online store chat

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store** > [**Themes**](https://admin.shopify.com/themes).
2. Click **Edit theme** next to the theme that you want to edit.
3. Select **App embeds**, and then click **Shopify Inbox**.
4. In the **Shopify Inbox** section, toggle on the **Online store chat** button.
5. Click **Save**.

## Add a profile picture

You can personalize your online store chat appearance by adding a profile picture to Shopify Inbox. Your Shopify Inbox avatar uses the picture from your account settings. You can change your profile picture by updating your [account details](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-account) in your Shopify profile.

#### Steps:

1. From [Shopify Inbox](https://inbox.shopify.com/), go to **Settings** > **Message preferences**.
2. In the **Avatar** section, check **Use admin profile picture**.

## Customize your brand, chat button, and greeting message

From your theme editor, you can personalize the look of the Shopify Inbox chat button, its location on your online store, and your chat greeting message.

#### Steps:

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store** > [**Themes**](https://admin.shopify.com/themes).
2. Click **Edit theme** next to the theme that you want to edit.
3. Select **App embeds**, and then click **Shopify Inbox**.
4. In the **Brand customization** section, do any of the following:
	- Click **Color** to access the color picker, then select or enter a hexadecimal code for your background, text, or button color.
		- From the **Icon** drop-down menu, select the icon that you want to display for the chat button on your online store. You can choose from the following icons: **Chat bubble**, **Email**, **Question mark**, **Smiley face**, **Team**, **Hand wave**, or **No icon**.
		- From the **Label** drop-down menu, select the label that you want to display for the chat button on your online store. You can choose from the following labels: **Chat**, **Assistance**, **Contact**, **Help**, **Support**, **Live chat**, **Message us**, **Need help?**, or **No text**.
		- From the **Horizontal position** and **Vertical position** sections, select the position where you want the chat button to display on your online store.
		- In the **Greeting message** text box, enter the message that you want to display to your customer when they click on the chat button.
5. Click **Save**.
