## Accurate accounting for retail and B2B sales, payments, and product details

QuickBooks provides accounting software for Shopify retail and B2B merchants that brings sales activity, payments, customers, and product details into one centralized place for smoother bookkeeping and easier oversight. Categories are mapped for you to reduce manual entry and help keep your books accurate as you sell and your shop evolves. Connect your store at no extra cost — the connector is included.
