Master Software Development Agreement (Pro-customer)

A master services agreement for the procurement of software development and related services. While drafted in favor of the customer, this Standard Document aims to be reasonable and includes provisions commonly negotiated in software development agreements. This Standard Document has integrated notes with important explanations and drafting and negotiating tips.
